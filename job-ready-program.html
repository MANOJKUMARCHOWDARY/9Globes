FAQ's

Who is eligible for this program?

Any Btech/BE /MTech final year and passed outs are eligible for this program.

What is the duration of the program?

It is a 16 week program. After the completion of the program, the student gets paid internship for 2 Months and then placements.

Do I get the assured placement?

Yes

What is the basic academic percentage required to enroll for the course?

60% in btech/BE/ Mtech is eligible to enroll for the course.

What is the execution plan of the program?

2 Hours Lecture + 2 Hours of Practice + 2 Hours of Assignments + 2 Hours of Exploration

Can we take this course online?

Yes, This course is applicable for offline and online students.

I am already employed, will I be eligible for the program?

No. Only students looking for a job as a fresher is eligible for the program.

Due to an emergency, If I miss the session?

You will get a recording of the live session.

Do you provide any certificate after the program?

You get a course completion certificate and internship certificate from DigitalLync.
